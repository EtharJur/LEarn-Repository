Challenges

Embark on your learning journey! Complete challenges to unlock new levels of knowledge and skills.

Article Explorer Challenge

  • Read three articles of your choice from the provided list.
  • Gain diverse perspectives on the course topics.
  • Summarize each article in a paragraph, highlighting key points.

Quiz Mastery Challenge

  • Complete quizzes for the three recent lessons.
  • Reinforce your understanding of the course material.
  • Achieve a score of 80% or higher in each quiz.

Lesson Application Quest

  • Choose any three lessons and apply the concepts to a real-world scenario.
  • Demonstrate practical application and problem-solving skills.
  • Create a visually engaging infographic summarizing your applications.

Triple Learning Combo

  • Read one article, complete one quiz, and review one lesson in a single study session.
  • Integrate various learning formats for a comprehensive understanding.
  • Reflect on your learning experience in a short journal entry.
